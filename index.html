Gadsisar Sagar Lake

Have you enjoyed your holiday?

Gadsisar Sagar Lake, located in Jaisalmer, Rajasthan, is a stunning artificial lake built in the 14th century by Maharaja Gadsi Singh. This serene lake is surrounded by beautiful temples and ghats, making it a popular spot for tourists. The tranquil waters and rich history provide a perfect setting for relaxation and reflection. Visitors can also enjoy boating and bird watching at this picturesque location.
